Who Am I?

A researcher and creative technologist working at the intersection of VR, computer vision, and digital art. I connect creative practice with quantitative methods to produce immersive, data-driven experiences.

🌐

VR & Immersive Media

Designing interactive virtual environments — from VR-based driving training systems to museum installations — using Unreal Engine, Unity, and custom ML pipelines.

✨
